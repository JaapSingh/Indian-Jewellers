INDIAN JEWELLERS

Bringing you the finest gold and silver jewellery from South-East Asia.

Curating to your every need, Indian Jewellers is a store built on two principles: respect and quality. Owned and run by Naginder Gill, Indian Jewellers has been open for 2 years and is dedicated to bringing you the finest jewellery from south east Asia. Ideally located along airport road, we at Indian Jewellers promise to provide you with jewellery of the highest purity and greatest variety. We are happy to provide all of our customers with same day repairs and resizings, all at affordable prices. With an industrial spectrometer built specifically for testing jewellery, we at Indian Jewellers can confirm the purity of gold and silver jewellery down to the last carrot. Additionally, we provide our customers with many other services such as on the spot live gold prices, exact weights using premium weighing machines, and we even accept debit and visa cards. At Indian Jewellers, our goal is to be unique and special, so we provide many different East Indian gold items including lion claws, Mungul-Sutras, and Khandas.

With jewellery sets, bangles, neclaces, rings and even 24-K bars, we at Indian Jewellers are able to provide our customers with a wide variety of Indian jewellery. Also specialising in custom work, Indian Jewellers is the perfect place to find a truly unique piece just for you. So please, come visit us at our Malton location, and discover the wonderful world of India.
